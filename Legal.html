information about our use of cookies

This section outlines the purpose and utilization of cookies by fG group ("We"). Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to identify you and your preferences, optimize the performance of our website, and gather analytical data for the benefit of our subscribers and business partners. The term "cookies" encompasses comparable technologies for gathering and storing information, such as Local Shared Objects (often referred to as "flash cookies") and web beacons or web bugs (including transparent or clear gifs).

Session cookies are utilized to monitor your activities within a single browser session, allowing us to remember items from a search, but they do not persist beyond the session. On the other hand, persistent cookies remain on your device between sessions, enabling us to authenticate you and recall your preferences. They also aid in balancing server loads, thus enhancing your website experience. Both session and persistent cookies can be either first-party or third-party cookies - first-party cookies are set by the website you visit, while third-party cookies come from a different website. At http://www.fg.com, we utilize a combination of first-party, session, and persistent cookies to deliver efficient services and improve your experience. The third-party cookies we employ collect anonymized information. We do not utilize behavioral targeting cookies. All our cookies fall within the ICC classifications of Strictly Necessary, Functionality, and Performance, and none fall under the category of Behavioral Targeting. We consider these cookies essential for the efficient operation of our website and to provide you with the best possible service. If you wish to disable our cookies at any time, you can do so through your browser settings. However, please note that by doing so, certain important features of our service may become unavailable to you.
